Ginger Bread Man

The Gingerbread Man is a character from the fairy tale "The Gingerbread Man" and appears in the Shrek franchise. He is known for his ability to run very fast and his role as a minor antagonist in the first film. Despite being a minor character, he is memorable for his distinctive appearance and his role in the story.
